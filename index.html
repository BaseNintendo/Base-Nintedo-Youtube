Base-Nintedo-Youtube by BaseNintendo

All Of our intro's, overlays, and more!

Install

The .veg are vegas files, they will not work with any other programs except for sony vegas. For everyone that does not have sony vegas, use the .gif file and the .png file. Place the .gif at the beggining of your video with the .png right behind it. Then, streach the .png to the end of your video. For the .veg files, just stretch the top layer to the end of your video. MAKE SURE THAT YOU HAVE THE WATERMARKS/OVERLAYS ON THE TOP TRACK OF YOUR VIDEO.
